Generated Brief

EMIT imaged a coherent methane plume over the Goturdepe gas field on 15 Aug 2022. Our independent matched-filter retrieval reproduces NASA's enhancement at r = 0.75 and yields 41.2 t integrated mass over a 192.6 km² mask. With a 6.9 m/s wind this implies ≈27 t CH₄/hr from this single source — one of twelve Thorpe et al. quantify at 163 ± 18 t/hr.
